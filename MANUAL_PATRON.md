# LLORA — Patron Guide

## Your Library's AI Assistant

**LLORA** (Lorma Library Online Research Assistant) is a virtual assistant available on the Lorma Colleges Library website. LLORA is here to help you find books, get answers about library services, and connect you with a librarian — anytime the library is open.

---

## How to Open the Chat

Look for the **LLORA avatar button** in the bottom-right corner of the library website. Click it to open the chat window.

When you open the chat for the first time, you will be asked to identify yourself:

- **Student (Higher Ed)** — college students
- **Student (Basic Ed)** — junior high / senior high students
- **Faculty / Staff** — teachers and university employees
- **Alumni** — graduates of Lorma Colleges
- **Visitor** — guests and walk-in patrons

Select your patron type and add any relevant details (e.g. your course and year level), then click **Start Chat**.

---

## What LLORA Can Help You With

### 📚 Finding Books

Ask LLORA to search the library catalog for you. You can search by:

- **Title** — "Do you have Harry Potter?"
- **Author** — "Books by Jose Rizal"
- **Subject** — "I need books about computer networking"
- **Course material** — "Textbooks for BSIT students"

LLORA will show you a list of matching books with links to view each one in the catalog.

**Example questions:**
> "Find me books about data structures"
> "Do you have any books by Stephen Hawking?"
> "I need a reference book for calculus"

---

### 🕐 Library Hours and Location

Ask LLORA about library schedules and where to find us.

**Example questions:**
> "What are the library hours?"
> "Is the library open on Saturday?"
> "Where is the library located?"

---

### 📋 Policies and Borrowing Rules

Ask about borrowing limits, loan periods, renewals, and membership.

**Example questions:**
> "How many books can I borrow?"
> "How long can I keep a book?"
> "Can I renew my borrowed books?"
> "How do I get a library card?"

---

### 💸 Fines and Fees

Ask about overdue fines and other charges.

**Example questions:**
> "What is the fine for overdue books?"
> "How much is the overdue fee per day?"

---

### 🖨️ Printing Services

Ask about printing rates and how to print documents at the library.

**Example questions:**
> "How much does printing cost?"
> "What are the printing rates?"
> "How do I print a document at the library?"

---

### 📧 Contact Information

Ask for the library's email address or contact details.

**Example questions:**
> "What is the library's email address?"
> "How do I contact the library?"

---

## Quick Reply Buttons

When you open the chat, you will see a row of **quick reply buttons** at the bottom of the chat window. These are shortcuts to the most common questions — just click one to get an instant answer without typing.

---

## Talking to a Librarian

If LLORA cannot answer your question, or if you prefer to speak with a real person, click the **Librarian** button in the chat header.

**What happens next:**

1. LLORA will notify the available librarians immediately
2. You will see: *"I've notified a librarian — they'll join this chat shortly!"*
3. A librarian will join the chat and respond directly
4. When the librarian is done, they will end the chat and you will be returned to LLORA

> **Note:** The Librarian button is only available during library opening hours. It is automatically disabled 30 minutes before closing time.

### Cancelling a librarian request

If you change your mind before a librarian joins, click **Cancel Request** to go back to chatting with LLORA.

### Rating your experience

After a librarian ends the chat, you will be asked to rate your experience on a scale of 1 to 4. Your feedback helps us improve the service.

---

## Tips for Better Results

- **Be specific** — "Books about Python programming for beginners" works better than "programming books"
- **Try different words** — if LLORA doesn't find what you need, try rephrasing your question
- **Use the quick reply buttons** — for common questions about hours, fines, and policies, the buttons give instant answers
- **Ask follow-up questions** — LLORA remembers your conversation, so you can say "tell me more about the first one" after a book search

---

## What LLORA Cannot Help With

LLORA is designed specifically for library-related topics. It will politely decline to answer questions about:

- General knowledge or trivia
- Homework answers or math problems
- Current events or news
- Anything unrelated to the library or school

For those topics, please use a general search engine or ask your teacher.

---

## Privacy

Your chat session is anonymous. You are identified only by a randomly generated name (like "Blue Owl 42") — your real name is never stored. The patron type you select at the start is only shared with librarians if you request a live chat.

---

## Need More Help?

If you have a question LLORA cannot answer, use the **Librarian** button to connect with a real librarian, or visit the library in person during opening hours.
